What is Zumba?

Zumba is a high-energy fitness program that combines Latin and international music with dance moves. It incorporates interval training—alternating fast and slow rhythms—to help improve cardiovascular fitness. Created in the 1990s, Zumba has become a global phenomenon, offering a fun and engaging way to work out.

With easy-to-follow choreography, Zumba is suitable for all fitness levels and guarantees an exciting and effective workout.

The Origin of Zumba

Zumba was created by Colombian dancer and choreographer Alberto "Beto" Pérez in the 1990s. It started as a happy accident when Beto forgot his usual aerobics music and improvised with Latin dance tracks. This blend of fitness and fun quickly gained popularity, transforming into a global fitness revolution.

Benefits of Zumba

Improved Fitness

Zumba improves cardiovascular health, endurance, and overall physical fitness.

Stress Relief

Dance your stress away with energizing music and positive vibes.

Join a global movement and connect with others through dance and fitness.
